The Snow Plower
Mama's description
The Snow Plower finds opportunities when it may seem hard to do so. It does not happen regularly, but it likes to show of its strength in times when no one else dares.

This bot trades quick mean-reversions in small coins. Moreover it only trades when a coin is considered strong to increase probability of reversion.

Technical description
This bot is part of the Reversals and Pullbacks (RAP) bot series. It finds possibilities in markets that tend to move a lot and have a large volatility.

Using carefully calculated supply and demand zones it can anticipate on price action early. It's purpose is to generate profits no matter the general market direction.
