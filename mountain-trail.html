Mama's description
The Mountain Trail is a trail with exciting curves with a sun shining from high mountain peaks on routes along deep precipices. It trades coins with a relatively small sized volume. It does not try to predict anything fancy, it rides with the coins for a while as they go up in value.

The Mountain Trail is designed in such a way that it can withstand steep hills, volatile market conditions and having an appurtenant risk management system.
Technicals
Technical description
This bot is a relatively simple but strong algo. It uses a reversed engineered Relative Strength Index (RSI) to determine the price range, applied on three different timeframes.

A mathematical model analyses the relative direction of the price per timeframe. Once the direction for all three timeframes align, the bot decides to take a position.
